## Description
We've added all kinds of constants available in Google Sheets, like:
- physical constants - electromagnetic constants - atomic and nuclear constants - physico-chemical constants The digits in between brackets (see reference) are left out and sometimes the significance is reduced to stay within the 15 digits cell capacity. Very small values, like the Planck length, are best formatted in a scientific notation to make the decimals visible. The decimals are still accounted for when doing calculations. If you want to have more constants added, use the [Google Forms][1] on our website. ## Syntax and Usage CONST_PLANCKLENGTH() or CONSTANTS(1) or CONSTANTS(A1:A5) Outputs the Planck length constant.
